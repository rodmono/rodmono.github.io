About

I am currently the Head Quant at Ratio Finance, a collateralized debt position protocol in the Solana Blockchain. I was previously working for the Emerging Markets Desk as XVA Trader and Front Office Quant at Scotiabank Global Banking and Markets. I started my career in finance at Grupo Bursatil Mexicano where I learned, created and implemented quantitative trading strategies for the futures and equities markets

I got involved with cryptoassets in 2017 and I decided to go all-in in 2022. I am mainly interested in developing data-driven quantitive solutions for investor portfolio creation and financial risk management. I enjoy bridging the gap between theoretical research and real-world financial applications attempting to add some value to the industry.

  • BSc and MSc - Theoretical Physics at UNAM (2013)
