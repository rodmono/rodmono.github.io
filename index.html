About

I work at Block Analitica as a Researcher and Risk Analyst. The main focus of my research is Counterparty Credit Risk and Market Risk.

Previously I was the Head Quant at Ratio Finance, a collateralized debt position protocol in the Solana Blockchain. I also worked at at Scotiabank Global Banking and Marketsfor the Emerging Markets Desk as XVA Trader and Front Office Quant. I started my career in Finance at Grupo Bursatil Mexicano where I learned, created and implemented quantitative trading strategies for futures and equities.

I got involved with cryptoassets in 2017 and I decided to go all-in in 2022. I am mainly interested in developing data-driven quantitive solutions for investor portfolio creation and financial risk management. I enjoy bridging the gap between theoretical research and real-world financial applications attempting to add some value to the industry.
